What elections should I look for?
Our dropdown includes candidate and voter power information for the Presidential race, Senate races, House of Representatives' races, Gubernatorial races, State Legislature's races, and Ballot measures. Different races have different voter powers, and for a given area, our website defines these individually. All of this information can be observed by using the dropdown on a relevant state or by searching a local address. Note that more elections will appear on your ballot apart from these races. Our database is not an exhaustive list of every election race in one area.
Why is [insert election/candidate here] missing?
Our data collection process is limited by certain factors - some states finish their primaries much sooner than others, candidates drop out, and polling data is sometimes unavailable. We endeavor to get as much information as possible, for more complete pictures of the US electoral map.
